I am a young and experimenting artist, manipulating any medium I can get my hands on. I have been fascinated by the Golden Ratio for a number of years. By creating my own interpretations of this aesthetically pleasing formula, I aim to evoke emotion. I utilize the skills of incorporating motion and colour in my art to guide the viewers eye around the piece. I focus on balance and harmony to create a dynamic ambiance in all my work.
